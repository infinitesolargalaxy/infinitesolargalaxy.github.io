About Me

Infatuated by Science Fiction and the Changing World.

I am a Web Developer at ModiFace. I believe that Code and Programs are an expression of Creativity. In my free time, I enjoy modding video games, writing science-fiction stories, reading, creating spreadsheets, and cycling.

My name is Michael Ly, and I am fortunate to be born in Canada! My dream is to travel to the Stars above, to join humanity at the next frontier.

As that dream remains at the edge of science-fiction for now, I strive to create my own worlds through code, artwork, and stories.
