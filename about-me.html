About Me

Infatuated by Science Fiction and the Changing World.

My name is Michael Ly, and I am fortunate to be born in Canada! Currently, I am a Web Developer at a Beauty-tech company with 3 years of experience. I personally believe that Code and Programs are an expression of Creativity.

In my free time, I enjoy modding video games, writing science-fiction stories, reading, creating spreadsheets, and cycling.

As for my inspiration: I grew up watching the Star Wars Trilogy over and over again, and playing a Nintendo DS game called Infinite Space. My lifelong dream is to travel to the Stars above, to join humanity at the next frontier.

As that dream remains at the edge of science-fiction for now, I strive to create my own worlds through code, artwork, and stories.
